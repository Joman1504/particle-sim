Poster:

    1-page PDF
    A concise visual summary of the project, with a clean layout, readable text, and purposeful visuals that emphasize the problem, methods, results, and key takeaway rather than long paragraphs. Please google "research poster" to learn some decent research posters' layout and content.

Poster = 5 points

    1 point: project idea
    1 point: methods / pipeline
    1 point: results / timing evidence
    1 point: visual clarity
    1 point: key takeaway
